## The brief

Build a voice acting and sound design portfolio that lets casting directors and producers evaluate range fast — character work, commercial reads, and full audio production — while signaling a broadcast-ready studio and quick turnaround.

## What I built

A portfolio centered on audio samples and credits. Clear service breakdown across voice acting and sound design, prominent client list, and studio specs to telegraph production quality. Built for speed so demos load instantly on first visit.

## Highlights

- **Audio-first** — reels and samples are the hero, not a wall of text
- **Credit signal** — client logos and project list build trust on the scroll
- **Studio specs** — mic, interface, and remote-record options surfaced for booking confidence

## Stack

Next.js, Tailwind CSS, deployed on Vercel.
