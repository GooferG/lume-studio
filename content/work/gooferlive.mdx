## The brief

Build a home base for a gambling streamer — centralize VODs and clips, surface gamba tools used during live streams, and showcase the full Steam library for gaming content.

## What I built

A streamer-focused site with a curated VOD and clips archive, integrated gambling tools, and a browsable Steam library breakdown. Built for speed and easy navigation across content types.

## Highlights

- **Content hub** — VODs and clips organized for quick browsing
- **Gamba tools** — dedicated section for gambling stream tools and resources
- **Steam library** — full game library breakdown for gaming stream discovery

## Stack

Next.js, Tailwind CSS, deployed on Vercel.
