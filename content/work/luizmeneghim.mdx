## The brief

A personal site that goes beyond a resume — tell the story of the journey, highlight real projects, and give visitors a clear sense of who built it and how they think.

## What I built

A portfolio site centered on narrative: a timeline of the journey, a projects section with real depth, and a design that reflects personal taste rather than a template. Fast, accessible, and easy to maintain.

## Highlights

- **Journey-first** — structured around growth and story, not just a skills list
- **Project depth** — each project gets real context, not just a screenshot and a title
- **Personal voice** — design and copy that feel human, not corporate

## Stack

Next.js, Tailwind CSS, deployed on Vercel.
